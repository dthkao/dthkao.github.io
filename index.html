BIOGRAPHY

I am a postdoctoral research associate under Prof. Salman Avestimehr.

I received my Ph.D. in May 2012 and my M.S. in May 2008 both in Electrical Engineering from Rice University where I was a part of the Center for Multimedia Communication under Prof. Ashutosh Sabharwal. I received my B.S. from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in May 2006.
