ABOUT

I'm at Google.

I received my Ph.D. in May 2012 and my M.S. in May 2008 both in Electrical Engineering from Rice University where I was a part of the Center for Multimedia Communication. I received my B.S. in May 2006 from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

From 2013 to 2015, I was a postdoctoral research associate in the Avestimehr Group --- first at Cornell University and then at the University of Southern California --- conducting research in Information Theory and Communications Networks. From March to July 2015, I was at Pixels.IO, working on innovative Visual Light Communications techology.
